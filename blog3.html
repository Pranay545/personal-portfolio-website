My Journey to UWC Maastricht

My journey to UWC Maastricht has been quite a ride! It all started last year when I decided to apply. I had to answer some tough questions from my favorite author and pretend to be the school principal solving problems. It made me think a lot about who I am and what I want to do.

After that, I got through to the next stage, where I had an online selection day. One task was to make a book that showed something important about me. I chose to show how I don't like to procrastinate and how I always try to be proactive. There's this Sindhi saying, "Andhan Multan Ladho," which means "Conquer the Impossible," and it really inspired me to keep going, no matter what.

UWC is all about bringing people together from different backgrounds to learn and understand each other. It's about making the world a better place through education. Now, I'm super excited to say that I've been accepted into UWC Maastricht! I'll be starting the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme this fall.

I can't wait to be part of a community where everyone's voice matters and where we can learn from each other's differences. This journey has taught me a lot about resilience and the power of diversity. I'm ready to take on whatever challenges come my way and make a positive difference in the world. UWC, here I come!
